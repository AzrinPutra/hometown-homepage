Top three activities to do at Singapore

Go for a walk in a local park

Jurong Lake gardens is a serene park with stunning views spanning Jurong lake and its skyline.

Go for a session of go-karting

Get your adrenaline pumping with a go-kart race at Singapore's largest local track @ KF1.

Go for a plate of Chicken Rice

Wee Nam Kee chicken rice will definitely satisfy any hunger pangs after a day out with its succlulent chicken.
